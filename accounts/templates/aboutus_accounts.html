About EKISAN

Welcome to EKISAN, your trusted platform for connecting farmers and dealers.

EKISAN is dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural trade by providing a seamless online marketplace where farmers can sell their products and dealers can place bids directly. Our platform aims to bridge the gap between farmers and markets, ensuring fair pricing and efficient transactions.

Founded in 2023, EKISAN is headquartered in Akola, Maharashtra, India. We strive to empower farmers by leveraging technology to simplify the process of selling agricultural products, thereby enhancing their livelihoods and contributing to agricultural growth.

Our Mission

EKISAN's mission is to create a transparent and efficient marketplace that empowers farmers and facilitates fair trade practices. We are committed to leveraging technology to enhance agricultural commerce and promote sustainable farming practices.
