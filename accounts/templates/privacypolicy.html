Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to EKISAN, a platform dedicated to connecting farmers and dealers, enabling farmers to sell their products online and dealers to place bids on these products. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information:

  • Farmers: When you register as a farmer, we collect information such as your name, address, state, city, pincode, mobile number, email address, and other relevant details necessary to facilitate your product listings and transactions.
  • Dealers: When you register as a dealer, we collect similar personal information to ensure smooth communication and transaction processes.

2. Transaction Information:

  • Details of the products listed by farmers and the bids placed by dealers.
  • Payment and transaction history.

3. Usage Data:

Information on how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent on the site, and other usage metrics.

How We Use Your Information

1. To Facilitate Transactions:

We use your personal and transaction information to connect farmers and dealers, enabling the sale and purchase of agricultural products.

2. To Improve Our Services:

Usage data helps us understand user behavior and improve the functionality and user experience of our platform.

3. Communication:

We use your contact information to send important updates, notifications, and promotional materials related to our services.

4. Compliance and Legal Obligations:

We may use your information to comply with legal requirements and regulations, or to respond to lawful requests and legal processes.

Sharing Your Information

1. With Other Users:

Relevant personal information is shared between farmers and dealers to facilitate transactions. For example, a dealer may need to contact a farmer to discuss a product listing or bid.

2. With Third-Party Service Providers:

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, such as payment processors and hosting services. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it solely for the purposes specified by EKISAN.

3. For Legal Reasons:

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Despite our efforts, no data transmission over the internet or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Therefore, we encourage you to take precautions to protect your personal data when you use our services.

Your Rights

1. Access and Update:

You have the right to access and update your personal information at any time through your account settings.

2. Delete:

You can request the deletion of your personal information, subject to our need to retain certain information for legal or administrative purposes.

3. Opt-Out:

You may opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the instructions in those communications or by updating your account settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the effective date.
